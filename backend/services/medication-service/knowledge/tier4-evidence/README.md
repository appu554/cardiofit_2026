# TIER 4 - Evidence & Quality

## 🎯 **Purpose**
Evidence-based rationale and quality measures that support clinical decisions with scientific backing.

## 📋 **Components**

### **Evidence Repository (ER)**
- Clinical guidelines and protocols
- Medical literature references
- Professional society recommendations
- Safety data and warnings

### **Quality Metrics**
- Performance indicators
- Audit requirements
- Compliance measures
- Outcome tracking

## 🔗 **Integration Points**
- **Recommendation Engine**: Uses ER for clinical rationale
- **ORB Engine**: Uses ER for rule validation
- **Audit System**: Uses quality metrics
- **Clinical Decision Support**: Uses evidence for explanations

## 📚 **Evidence Sources**
- **Professional Societies**: AHA, ACC, IDSA, etc.
- **Government Agencies**: FDA, CDC, NIH
- **Clinical Guidelines**: Evidence-based protocols
- **Medical Literature**: Peer-reviewed studies

## 🎯 **Value**
- **Traceability**: Every decision has evidence backing
- **Compliance**: Meets regulatory requirements
- **Quality**: Continuous improvement metrics
- **Trust**: Clinicians see the evidence basis

**Ensures all clinical intelligence is evidence-based and auditable.**
