CKM Stage 4 Subcategorization — Implementation Plan
Clinical Rationale
Why this is architectural debt that compounds over time:
The entire CardioFit platform is built on the AHA's Cardiovascular-Kidney-Metabolic (CKM) syndrome framework, published by Ndumele et al. in Circulation (October 2023). CKM staging is the backbone — it determines card urgency, therapy pathways, phenotype clustering context, MHRI interpretation, and dual-domain classification. Every clinical decision flows through ckm_stage.
The current implementation stores CKM stage as an integer 0-4. For Stages 0-3, this is adequate because the clinical pathways within each stage are relatively homogeneous. Stage 4 is fundamentally different. The AHA framework explicitly defines three substages with distinct pathophysiology, prognosis, and treatment strategies:
Stage 4a — Subclinical CVD in CKM syndrome: The patient has metabolic risk factors (obesity, T2DM, hypertension, CKD) PLUS subclinical cardiovascular disease detected by imaging or biomarkers, but NO symptomatic cardiovascular events. Subclinical CVD includes: coronary artery calcification (CAC) score >0, carotid intima-media thickness (cIMT) >75th percentile, left ventricular hypertrophy (LVH) on echo or ECG, elevated natriuretic peptides (NT-proBNP >125 pg/mL) without clinical HF, or subclinical atherosclerosis on CT angiography.
Clinical significance of 4a: These patients are in a window where aggressive risk factor modification can prevent progression to clinical events. The MESA study (Multi-Ethnic Study of Atherosclerosis) showed that CAC >100 in a diabetic patient carries a 10-year CVD event rate of 18.6% — aggressive statin therapy, BP control to <130/80, and SGLT2i/GLP-1 RA for cardiorenal protection can halve this. The medication strategy is fundamentally preventive: maximize cardioprotective agents before the first event.
Stage 4b — Clinical CVD equivalent in CKM syndrome: The patient has had a clinical cardiovascular event: myocardial infarction, stroke/TIA, peripheral arterial disease, coronary revascularization (PCI/CABG), or documented significant coronary artery disease (≥50% stenosis). This is secondary prevention — the horse has left the barn, and the goal shifts to preventing recurrence.
Clinical significance of 4b: The medication strategy is fundamentally different from 4a. Mandatory components include: high-intensity statin (atorvastatin 40-80mg), antiplatelet therapy (aspirin ± P2Y12 inhibitor), aggressive BP control (<130/80 with ACEi/ARB preferred), and beta-blocker if post-MI. SGLT2i is now indicated for cardiovascular risk reduction independent of glycaemic status (EMPA-REG, CANVAS, DECLARE). GLP-1 RA with proven CV benefit (liraglutide, semaglutide, dulaglutide) is preferred over agents without CV outcome data. The dual-domain card generator must recognize that a 4b patient needs secondary prevention agents regardless of current HbA1c or BP levels.
Stage 4c — Heart failure in CKM syndrome (HF-CKM): The patient has heart failure in the context of CKM syndrome. This subdivides further into HFrEF (reduced ejection fraction, EF ≤40%), HFmrEF (mildly reduced, EF 41-49%), and HFpEF (preserved, EF ≥50%). CKM-associated HF is overwhelmingly HFpEF — the obesity-diabetes-hypertension triad drives diastolic dysfunction and HFpEF in >60% of CKM Stage 4c patients (Borlaug et al., 2023, JACC).
Clinical significance of 4c: This is where the medication strategy diverges most dramatically. HFrEF has the "four pillars of HF therapy" (ACEi/ARB/ARNI, beta-blocker, MRA, SGLT2i) — all four are mandatory, evidence-based, and guideline-directed (AHA/ACC/HFSA 2022). HFpEF has historically had no proven therapies until EMPEROR-Preserved (2021) and DELIVER (2022) showed SGLT2i benefit — now SGLT2i is the ONLY guideline-recommended disease-modifying therapy for HFpEF. The renal dose gating system must know whether a patient is 4c-HFrEF vs 4c-HFpEF because the mandatory medication list is completely different. A card recommending beta-blocker intensification for a 4c-HFpEF patient is not just unhelpful — it may worsen exercise intolerance.
Why integer 0-4 is dangerous:
Consider two patients both at ckm_stage: 4:
* Patient A: 62yo male, T2DM, HTN, eGFR 45, CAC score 350, no prior events (Stage 4a). Strategy: maximize prevention — SGLT2i, GLP-1 RA, high-intensity statin, aggressive BP control.
* Patient B: 58yo female, T2DM, HTN, prior MI 2 years ago, EF 35%, NYHA Class II (Stage 4c-HFrEF). Strategy: four pillars of HF therapy — sacubitril/valsartan (NOT standard ACEi/ARB), carvedilol, spironolactone, dapagliflozin. Metformin dose limited by eGFR. Avoid pioglitazone (fluid retention in HF). Avoid saxagliptin (SAVOR-TIMI 53 showed increased HF hospitalization).
The current system treats both identically as "ckm_stage: 4" and would generate the same cards, apply the same phenotype clustering context, compute the same MHRI interpretation. This is a patient safety issue for Patient B — recommending pioglitazone or saxagliptin to a HFrEF patient because the system doesn't distinguish substages.
The AHA CKM staging criteria (Ndumele et al., 2023, Circulation):
Stage 0: No CKM risk factors. Normal BMI, normoglycaemic, normotensive, normal lipids, normal kidney function.
Stage 1: Excess adiposity (BMI ≥25, or ≥23 Asian) without metabolic derangement OR metabolic risk factors without excess adiposity.
Stage 2: Metabolic risk factors present — T2DM, HTN, hypertriglyceridaemia, metabolic syndrome. OR CKD (eGFR <60 or ACR ≥30). OR high-risk prediabetes.
Stage 3: Subclinical CVD absent, but high predicted ASCVD risk (≥7.5% 10-year by PCE, or PREVENT equation ≥10%). OR very high CKD risk (eGFR <30 or ACR ≥300).
Stage 4a: Subclinical ASCVD (CAC >0, cIMT >75th%ile, LVH, subclinical atherosclerosis) OR subclinical HF (elevated NT-proBNP, structural heart disease on echo without symptoms).
Stage 4b: Clinical ASCVD — MI, stroke, PAD, coronary revascularization, significant CAD.
Stage 4c: Heart failure (HFrEF, HFmrEF, or HFpEF) in CKM context. With or without concurrent ASCVD.
India-specific CKM considerations:
* Asian BMI cutoffs: Stage 1 threshold is BMI ≥23 (not 25) per WHO Asia-Pacific classification. Waist circumference thresholds: male ≥90cm, female ≥80cm (IDF Asian). This is already in the market config.
* ASCVD risk prediction: The Pooled Cohort Equations (PCE) overestimate risk in South Asian populations. The QRISK3 model (used in UK for South Asians) or India-specific risk equations (Joshi et al., 2016) are more appropriate but less validated. The system should use the PREVENT equation (AHA 2023) which replaces PCE and includes eGFR and ACR — directly integrating renal risk into CV prediction.
* Rheumatic heart disease: In India, a significant subset of Stage 4c patients have rheumatic valvular heart disease rather than CKM-driven HF. These patients need anticoagulation (warfarin with INR monitoring) and surgical evaluation — fundamentally different from CKM-HFpEF. The system should flag rheumatic etiology as a distinct pathway.
* Young-onset CVD: India has disproportionately high rates of MI in adults <45 years. Stage 4b in a 38-year-old Indian male carries different prognostic and therapeutic implications than in a 68-year-old — more aggressive secondary prevention, family screening for familial hypercholesterolaemia, longer treatment horizon.
Australia-specific CKM considerations:
* Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander populations: CVD onset is 10-20 years earlier. Acute rheumatic fever and rheumatic heart disease prevalence is among the highest globally. CKM Stage 4 classification must account for rheumatic etiology. NACCHO guidelines recommend screening echocardiography from age 5 in high-risk communities.
* NVDPA (National Vascular Disease Prevention Alliance) risk assessment: The Australian absolute CVD risk calculator is mandated by RACGP for Stage 3 assessment. Results should feed into CKM staging automatically.
* PBS implications of substaging: Stage 4b (post-MI) qualifies for PBS authority for ticagrelor, prasugrel, eplerenone. Stage 4c-HFrEF qualifies for PBS authority for sacubitril/valsartan (Entresto). Without substaging, the system cannot provide the correct PBS item codes — an administrative barrier to optimal prescribing.

Architecture Overview
The subcategorization touches every service that consumes ckm_stage:
1. KB-20 (Patient Profile): Change ckm_stage from int to structured type with stage + substage + metadata. Migration path from integer to structured type. Update ComputeCKMStage() to produce substages. New fields for CVD history, HF classification, LVEF, NT-proBNP.

2. KB-23 (Decision Cards): Card builder must branch on substage for Stage 4. Dual-domain classifier must weight domains differently by substage. Drug conflict detector must add HF-specific contraindications (pioglitazone in 4c, saxagliptin in 4c). New mandatory medication checklist per substage.

3. KB-26 (Metabolic Digital Twin): MHRI cardiovascular domain scoring must account for substage — 4a (preventive trajectory) vs 4b (recurrence risk) vs 4c (HF progression). CKM velocity computation needs substage-aware thresholds.

4. Flink (Module 3/4): Cross-domain CEP patterns need substage context. A hemodynamic deterioration event in a 4c-HFrEF patient has different urgency than in a 4a patient.

5. Phenotype Clustering: Cluster interpretation changes by substage — 4c-HFpEF patients cluster differently from 4b post-MI patients on the same feature set.

Data model change:
Current:  ckm_stage INTEGER (0-4)


Proposed: ckm_stage VARCHAR(5)  — "0", "1", "2", "3", "4a", "4b", "4c"
          ckm_substage_metadata JSONB — {
              "hf_type": "HFrEF" | "HFmrEF" | "HFpEF",  -- only for 4c
              "lvef_pct": 35,
              "ascvd_events": ["MI_2024", "PCI_2024"],    -- only for 4b
              "subclinical_markers": ["CAC_350", "LVH"],  -- only for 4a
              "nyha_class": "II",                          -- only for 4c
              "nt_probnp": 450,
              "staging_date": "2026-01-15",
              "staging_source": "CARDIOLOGIST",
              "rheumatic_etiology": false
          }


Migration strategy: The integer-to-string migration must be backward compatible. Existing ckm_stage: 4 records become ckm_stage: "4a" by default (subclinical assumed — safest default since it triggers the most conservative/preventive pathway). Clinician review then reclassifies to 4b or 4c based on clinical history. The migration adds a ckm_substage_review_needed boolean flag set to true for all existing Stage 4 patients.

// kb-20-patient-profile/internal/models/ckm_stage_v2.go
package models


import (
    "database/sql/driver"
    "encoding/json"
    "fmt"
    "time"
)


// CKMStageValue represents the full CKM stage with substage.
// Valid values: "0", "1", "2", "3", "4a", "4b", "4c"
type CKMStageValue string


const (
    CKMStage0  CKMStageValue = "0"
    CKMStage1  CKMStageValue = "1"
    CKMStage2  CKMStageValue = "2"
    CKMStage3  CKMStageValue = "3"
    CKMStage4a CKMStageValue = "4a"
    CKMStage4b CKMStageValue = "4b"
    CKMStage4c CKMStageValue = "4c"
)


// IsStage4 returns true for any Stage 4 substage.
func (s CKMStageValue) IsStage4() bool {
    return s == CKMStage4a || s == CKMStage4b || s == CKMStage4c
}


// NumericStage returns the integer portion (0-4) for backward compatibility.
func (s CKMStageValue) NumericStage() int {
    switch s {
    case CKMStage0:
        return 0
    case CKMStage1:
        return 1
    case CKMStage2:
        return 2
    case CKMStage3:
        return 3
    case CKMStage4a, CKMStage4b, CKMStage4c:
        return 4
    default:
        return -1
    }
}


// IsValid checks if the stage value is one of the defined constants.
func (s CKMStageValue) IsValid() bool {
    return s.NumericStage() >= 0
}


// HFType represents the heart failure classification for Stage 4c.
type HFType string


const (
    HFTypeReduced          HFType = "HFrEF"  // EF ≤ 40%
    HFTypeMildlyReduced    HFType = "HFmrEF" // EF 41-49%
    HFTypePreserved        HFType = "HFpEF"  // EF ≥ 50%
    HFTypeUnclassified     HFType = ""
)


// SubstageMetadata carries the clinical detail behind Stage 4 classification.
type SubstageMetadata struct {
    // Stage 4c — Heart Failure
    HFClassification  HFType   `json:"hf_type,omitempty"`
    LVEFPercent       *float64 `json:"lvef_pct,omitempty"`
    NYHAClass         string   `json:"nyha_class,omitempty"`        // I, II, III, IV
    NTproBNP          *float64 `json:"nt_probnp,omitempty"`         // pg/mL
    BNP               *float64 `json:"bnp,omitempty"`               // pg/mL
    HFEtiology        string   `json:"hf_etiology,omitempty"`       // ISCHAEMIC, DILATED, RHEUMATIC, HYPERTENSIVE, OTHER


    // Stage 4b — Clinical ASCVD
    ASCVDEvents       []ASCVDEvent `json:"ascvd_events,omitempty"`
    MostRecentEventDate *time.Time `json:"most_recent_event_date,omitempty"`
    OnAntiplatelet    bool         `json:"on_antiplatelet,omitempty"`


    // Stage 4a — Subclinical CVD
    SubclinicalMarkers []SubclinicalMarker `json:"subclinical_markers,omitempty"`
    CACScore           *float64            `json:"cac_score,omitempty"`
    CIMTPercentile     *int                `json:"cimt_percentile,omitempty"`
    HasLVH             bool                `json:"has_lvh,omitempty"`


    // Staging metadata
    StagingDate        time.Time  `json:"staging_date"`
    StagingSource      string     `json:"staging_source,omitempty"` // CARDIOLOGIST, ALGORITHM, GP
    ReviewNeeded       bool       `json:"review_needed,omitempty"` // true for migrated Stage 4 patients
    RheumaticEtiology  bool       `json:"rheumatic_etiology,omitempty"`
}


type ASCVDEvent struct {
    EventType string    `json:"event_type"` // MI, STROKE, TIA, PAD, PCI, CABG, SIGNIFICANT_CAD
    EventDate time.Time `json:"event_date"`
    Details   string    `json:"details,omitempty"`
}


type SubclinicalMarker struct {
    MarkerType string    `json:"marker_type"` // CAC, CIMT, LVH, NT_PROBNP_ELEVATED, SUBCLINICAL_ATHEROSCLEROSIS
    Value      string    `json:"value"`
    Date       time.Time `json:"date"`
}


// Scan implements sql.Scanner for GORM JSONB deserialization.
func (m *SubstageMetadata) Scan(value interface{}) error {
    if value == nil {
        return nil
    }
    bytes, ok := value.([]byte)
    if !ok {
        return fmt.Errorf("SubstageMetadata.Scan: expected []byte, got %T", value)
    }
    return json.Unmarshal(bytes, m)
}


// Value implements driver.Valuer for GORM JSONB serialization.
func (m SubstageMetadata) Value() (driver.Value, error) {
    if m.StagingDate.IsZero() && m.HFClassification == "" && len(m.ASCVDEvents) == 0 && len(m.SubclinicalMarkers) == 0 {
        return nil, nil
    }
    return json.Marshal(m)
}


// ClassifyHFType determines HF subtype from LVEF.
func ClassifyHFType(lvefPct float64) HFType {
    switch {
    case lvefPct <= 40:
        return HFTypeReduced
    case lvefPct <= 49:
        return HFTypeMildlyReduced
    default:
        return HFTypePreserved
    }
}


// CKMStageResult is the full staging output.
type CKMStageResult struct {
    Stage             CKMStageValue     `json:"stage"`
    PreviousStage     CKMStageValue     `json:"previous_stage,omitempty"`
    StageChanged      bool              `json:"stage_changed"`
    Metadata          SubstageMetadata  `json:"metadata"`
    StagingRationale  string            `json:"staging_rationale"`
}

    ADD COLUMN IF NOT EXISTS ckm_substage_review_needed BOOLEAN DEFAULT FALSE;


-- Step 2: Migrate existing integer ckm_stage to string
-- Stages 0-3 map directly; Stage 4 defaults to "4a" (safest: triggers preventive pathway)
UPDATE patient_profiles SET ckm_stage_v2 = CASE
    WHEN ckm_stage = 0 THEN '0'
    WHEN ckm_stage = 1 THEN '1'
    WHEN ckm_stage = 2 THEN '2'
    WHEN ckm_stage = 3 THEN '3'
    WHEN ckm_stage = 4 THEN '4a'
    ELSE '0'
END
WHERE ckm_stage_v2 IS NULL;


-- Step 3: Flag existing Stage 4 patients for clinician review
UPDATE patient_profiles
    SET ckm_substage_review_needed = TRUE,
        ckm_substage_metadata = jsonb_build_object(
            'review_needed', true,
            'staging_source', 'MIGRATION',
            'staging_date', NOW()
        )
WHERE ckm_stage = 4;


-- Step 4: Create indexes
CREATE INDEX IF NOT EXISTS idx_pp_ckm_v2 ON patient_profiles(ckm_stage_v2);
CREATE INDEX IF NOT EXISTS idx_pp_ckm_review ON patient_profiles(ckm_substage_review_needed)
    WHERE ckm_substage_review_needed = TRUE;

package services


import (
    "time"


    "kb-patient-profile/internal/models"
)


// CKMClassifierInput holds all data needed for CKM staging.
type CKMClassifierInput struct {
    // Demographics
    Age    int
    BMI    float64
    WaistCm float64
    Sex    string


    // Metabolic
    HasDiabetes    bool
    HasPrediabetes bool
    HasHTN         bool
    HasDyslipidemia bool
    HasMetSyndrome  bool
    HbA1c          *float64


    // Renal
    EGFR float64
    ACR  *float64 // mg/g


    // Cardiovascular risk
    ASCVD10YearRisk *float64 // PREVENT or PCE
    PREVENTScore    *float64 // AHA PREVENT equation (includes eGFR + ACR)


    // Subclinical CVD markers (Stage 4a)
    CACScore       *float64
    CIMTPercentile *int
    HasLVH         bool
    NTproBNP       *float64
    HasSubclinicalAtherosclerosis bool


    // Clinical CVD history (Stage 4b)
    ASCVDEvents []models.ASCVDEvent


    // Heart failure (Stage 4c)
    HasHeartFailure bool
    LVEF            *float64
    NYHAClass       string
    HFEtiology      string


    // Market-specific
    AsianBMICutoffs bool // use 23/25 instead of 25/30
    RheumaticEtiology bool
}


// ClassifyCKMStage computes the full CKM stage with substage.
// Implements Ndumele et al. 2023 (Circulation) staging algorithm.
func ClassifyCKMStage(input CKMClassifierInput) models.CKMStageResult {
    result := models.CKMStageResult{
        Metadata: models.SubstageMetadata{
            StagingDate:   time.Now(),
            StagingSource: "ALGORITHM",
        },
    }


    // Stage 4c: Heart failure — highest stage, check first
    if input.HasHeartFailure {
        result.Stage = models.CKMStage4c
        result.StagingRationale = "Heart failure in CKM context"


        if input.LVEF != nil {
            hfType := models.ClassifyHFType(*input.LVEF)
            result.Metadata.HFClassification = hfType
            result.Metadata.LVEFPercent = input.LVEF
            result.StagingRationale += " — " + string(hfType)
        }
        result.Metadata.NYHAClass = input.NYHAClass
        result.Metadata.NTproBNP = input.NTproBNP
        result.Metadata.HFEtiology = input.HFEtiology
        result.Metadata.RheumaticEtiology = input.RheumaticEtiology
        return result
    }


    // Stage 4b: Clinical ASCVD
    if len(input.ASCVDEvents) > 0 {
        result.Stage = models.CKMStage4b
        result.Metadata.ASCVDEvents = input.ASCVDEvents


        mostRecent := input.ASCVDEvents[0].EventDate
        for _, e := range input.ASCVDEvents[1:] {
            if e.EventDate.After(mostRecent) {
                mostRecent = e.EventDate
            }
        }
        result.Metadata.MostRecentEventDate = &mostRecent
        result.StagingRationale = "Clinical ASCVD history: " + input.ASCVDEvents[0].EventType
        return result
    }


    // Stage 4a: Subclinical CVD
    hasSubclinical := false
    var markers []models.SubclinicalMarker


    if input.CACScore != nil && *input.CACScore > 0 {
        hasSubclinical = true
        result.Metadata.CACScore = input.CACScore
        markers = append(markers, models.SubclinicalMarker{
            MarkerType: "CAC", Value: fmt.Sprintf("%.0f", *input.CACScore), Date: time.Now(),
        })
    }
    if input.CIMTPercentile != nil && *input.CIMTPercentile > 75 {
        hasSubclinical = true
        markers = append(markers, models.SubclinicalMarker{
            MarkerType: "CIMT", Value: fmt.Sprintf(">p%d", *input.CIMTPercentile), Date: time.Now(),
        })
    }
    if input.HasLVH {
        hasSubclinical = true
        result.Metadata.HasLVH = true
        markers = append(markers, models.SubclinicalMarker{
            MarkerType: "LVH", Value: "present", Date: time.Now(),
        })
    }
    if input.NTproBNP != nil && *input.NTproBNP > 125 && !input.HasHeartFailure {
        hasSubclinical = true
        result.Metadata.NTproBNP = input.NTproBNP
        markers = append(markers, models.SubclinicalMarker{
            MarkerType: "NT_PROBNP_ELEVATED", Value: fmt.Sprintf("%.0f pg/mL", *input.NTproBNP), Date: time.Now(),
        })
    }
    if input.HasSubclinicalAtherosclerosis {
        hasSubclinical = true
        markers = append(markers, models.SubclinicalMarker{
            MarkerType: "SUBCLINICAL_ATHEROSCLEROSIS", Value: "present", Date: time.Now(),
        })
    }


    if hasSubclinical {
        result.Stage = models.CKMStage4a
        result.Metadata.SubclinicalMarkers = markers
        result.StagingRationale = fmt.Sprintf("Subclinical CVD: %d marker(s) detected", len(markers))
        return result
    }


    // Stage 3: High predicted risk
    highRisk := false
    if input.PREVENTScore != nil && *input.PREVENTScore >= 10.0 {
        highRisk = true
    } else if input.ASCVD10YearRisk != nil && *input.ASCVD10YearRisk >= 7.5 {
        highRisk = true
    }
    if input.EGFR < 30 || (input.ACR != nil && *input.ACR >= 300) {
        highRisk = true // very high CKD risk
    }


    if highRisk && (input.HasDiabetes || input.HasHTN || input.HasDyslipidemia) {
        result.Stage = models.CKMStage3
        result.StagingRationale = "High predicted ASCVD risk with metabolic risk factors"
        return result
    }


    // Stage 2: Metabolic risk factors or CKD
    hasMetabolic := input.HasDiabetes || input.HasHTN || input.HasDyslipidemia || input.HasMetSyndrome
    hasCKD := input.EGFR < 60 || (input.ACR != nil && *input.ACR >= 30)


    if hasMetabolic || hasCKD {
        result.Stage = models.CKMStage2
        result.StagingRationale = "Metabolic risk factors and/or CKD present"
        return result
    }


    // Stage 1: Excess adiposity
    bmiOverweight := 25.0
    if input.AsianBMICutoffs {
        bmiOverweight = 23.0
    }
    if input.BMI >= bmiOverweight {
        result.Stage = models.CKMStage1
        result.StagingRationale = "Excess adiposity without metabolic derangement"
        return result
    }


    // Stage 0: No CKM risk factors
    result.Stage = models.CKMStage0
    result.StagingRationale = "No CKM risk factors identified"
    return result
}

type HFContraindication struct {
    DrugClass    string
    HFTypes      []models.HFType // empty means ALL HF types
    Reason       string
    SourceTrial  string
}


var hfContraindications = []HFContraindication{

        DrugClass: "PIOGLITAZONE",
        HFTypes:   nil, // ALL HF types
        Reason:    "Fluid retention exacerbates heart failure (FDA black box warning)",
        SourceTrial: "FDA_LABEL",
    },
    {
        DrugClass: "SAXAGLIPTIN",
        HFTypes:   nil, // ALL
        Reason:    "Increased heart failure hospitalization in SAVOR-TIMI 53",
        SourceTrial: "SAVOR-TIMI_53",
    },
    {
        DrugClass: "ALOGLIPTIN",
        HFTypes:   nil, // ALL
        Reason:    "Potential HF risk signal in EXAMINE trial",
        SourceTrial: "EXAMINE",
    },
    {
        DrugClass: "DILTIAZEM",
        HFTypes:   []models.HFType{models.HFTypeReduced, models.HFTypeMildlyReduced},
        Reason:    "Negative inotropic effect worsens systolic dysfunction",
        SourceTrial: "AHA_HFSA_2022",
    },
    {
        DrugClass: "VERAPAMIL",
        HFTypes:   []models.HFType{models.HFTypeReduced, models.HFTypeMildlyReduced},
        Reason:    "Negative inotropic effect worsens systolic dysfunction",
        SourceTrial: "AHA_HFSA_2022",
    },
    {
        DrugClass: "NON_DHP_CCB",
        HFTypes:   []models.HFType{models.HFTypeReduced, models.HFTypeMildlyReduced},
        Reason:    "Non-dihydropyridine CCBs have negative inotropic effect in reduced EF",
        SourceTrial: "ESC_2023",
    },
}


type HFMedicationGate struct {
    contraindications []HFContraindication
}


func NewHFMedicationGate() *HFMedicationGate {
    return &HFMedicationGate{contraindications: hfContraindications}
}


// CheckContraindication returns (blocked, reason) if the drug is contraindicated
// in the patient's HF type. Only applies to Stage 4c.
func (g *HFMedicationGate) CheckContraindication(
    drugClass string,
    ckmStage models.CKMStageValue,
    metadata *models.SubstageMetadata,
) (bool, string) {
    // Only gate for Stage 4c
    if ckmStage != models.CKMStage4c {
        return false, ""
    }


    hfType := models.HFTypeUnclassified
    if metadata != nil {
        hfType = metadata.HFClassification
    }


    for _, ci := range g.contraindications {
        if ci.DrugClass != drugClass {
            continue
        }


        // If HFTypes is empty, applies to ALL HF types
        if len(ci.HFTypes) == 0 {
            return true, fmt.Sprintf("CONTRAINDICATED in heart failure: %s (%s)", ci.Reason, ci.SourceTrial)
        }


        // Check if patient's HF type is in the contraindicated list
        for _, ht := range ci.HFTypes {

                return true, fmt.Sprintf("CONTRAINDICATED in %s: %s (%s)", hfType, ci.Reason, ci.SourceTrial)
            }
        }
    }

// kb-23-decision-cards/internal/services/mandatory_med_checker.go
package services


import (
    "fmt"


    "kb-decision-cards/internal/models"
)


// MandatoryMedGap represents a medication that should be present but isn't.
type MandatoryMedGap struct {
    MissingClass string `json:"missing_class"`
    Rationale    string `json:"rationale"`
    Urgency      string `json:"urgency"`       // IMMEDIATE for core HF pillars, URGENT for secondary prevention
    Alternative  string `json:"alternative,omitempty"`
    SourceTrial  string `json:"source_trial"`
}


type MandatoryMedChecker struct{}


func NewMandatoryMedChecker() *MandatoryMedChecker {
    return &MandatoryMedChecker{}
}


func (c *MandatoryMedChecker) CheckMandatory(
    stage models.CKMStageValue,
    metadata *models.SubstageMetadata,
    activeMedClasses []string,
) []MandatoryMedGap {
    activeSet := make(map[string]bool)
    for _, mc := range activeMedClasses {
        activeSet[mc] = true
    }


    // Normalize: ACEi or ARB counts as RAS blockade
    hasRAS := activeSet["ACEi"] || activeSet["ARB"] || activeSet["ARNI"] || activeSet["SACUBITRIL_VALSARTAN"]
    hasAntiplatelet := activeSet["ASPIRIN"] || activeSet["CLOPIDOGREL"] || activeSet["TICAGRELOR"] || activeSet["PRASUGREL"]
    hasBB := activeSet["BETA_BLOCKER"] || activeSet["BETA_BLOCKER_HF"] || activeSet["CARVEDILOL"] || activeSet["BISOPROLOL"]


    var gaps []MandatoryMedGap


    switch stage {
    case models.CKMStage4a:
        if !activeSet["STATIN"] {
            gaps = append(gaps, MandatoryMedGap{
                MissingClass: "STATIN",
                Rationale:    "All Stage 4a require high-intensity statin for subclinical CVD",
                Urgency:      "URGENT",
                SourceTrial:  "AHA_ACC_2018",
            })
        }


    case models.CKMStage4b:
        if !activeSet["STATIN"] {
            gaps = append(gaps, MandatoryMedGap{
                MissingClass: "STATIN",
                Rationale:    "High-intensity statin mandatory for secondary ASCVD prevention",
                Urgency:      "IMMEDIATE",
                SourceTrial:  "AHA_ACC_2018, CTT_2010",
            })
        }
        if !hasAntiplatelet {
            gaps = append(gaps, MandatoryMedGap{
                MissingClass: "ANTIPLATELET",
                Rationale:    "Antiplatelet therapy mandatory post-ASCVD event",
                Urgency:      "IMMEDIATE",
                SourceTrial:  "CAPRIE, CURE",
            })
        }
        if !hasRAS {
            gaps = append(gaps, MandatoryMedGap{
                MissingClass: "ACEi_OR_ARB",
                Rationale:    "RAS blockade for post-ASCVD cardioprotection + renoprotection",
                Urgency:      "URGENT",
                SourceTrial:  "HOPE, ONTARGET",
            })
        }


    case models.CKMStage4c:
        if metadata == nil {
            // No HF classification — flag for echo
            gaps = append(gaps, MandatoryMedGap{
                MissingClass: "ECHOCARDIOGRAM",
                Rationale:    "LVEF unknown — echocardiography needed to guide HF therapy",
                Urgency:      "IMMEDIATE",
            })
            return gaps
        }


        switch metadata.HFClassification {
        case models.HFTypeReduced:
            // Four pillars of HFrEF therapy
            if !activeSet["SGLT2i"] {
                gaps = append(gaps, MandatoryMedGap{
                    MissingClass: "SGLT2i",
                    Rationale:    "SGLT2i is a mandatory pillar of HFrEF therapy",
                    Urgency:      "IMMEDIATE",
                    SourceTrial:  "DAPA-HF, EMPEROR-Reduced",
                })
            }
            if !hasBB {
                gaps = append(gaps, MandatoryMedGap{
                    MissingClass: "BETA_BLOCKER_HF",
                    Rationale:    "Evidence-based beta-blocker (carvedilol/bisoprolol/metoprolol succinate) mandatory in HFrEF",
                    Urgency:      "IMMEDIATE",
                    SourceTrial:  "CIBIS-II, MERIT-HF, COPERNICUS",
                })
            }
            if !activeSet["MRA"] && !activeSet["SPIRONOLACTONE"] && !activeSet["EPLERENONE"] {
                gaps = append(gaps, MandatoryMedGap{
                    MissingClass: "MRA",
                    Rationale:    "MRA mandatory in HFrEF (if K+ <5.0 and eGFR ≥30)",
                    Urgency:      "IMMEDIATE",
                    SourceTrial:  "RALES, EMPHASIS-HF",
                })
            }
            // Check for ARNI upgrade opportunity
            if (activeSet["ACEi"] || activeSet["ARB"]) && !activeSet["ARNI"] && !activeSet["SACUBITRIL_VALSARTAN"] {
                gaps = append(gaps, MandatoryMedGap{
                    MissingClass: "ARNI_UPGRADE",
                    Rationale:    "Consider switching ACEi/ARB to sacubitril/valsartan — superior outcomes in HFrEF",
                    Urgency:      "URGENT",
                    Alternative:  "Continue ACEi/ARB if ARNI not tolerated or unavailable",
                    SourceTrial:  "PARADIGM-HF",
                })
            }


        case models.HFTypeMildlyReduced:
            if !activeSet["SGLT2i"] {
                gaps = append(gaps, MandatoryMedGap{
                    MissingClass: "SGLT2i",
                    Rationale:    "SGLT2i recommended in HFmrEF — benefit across EF spectrum",
                    Urgency:      "URGENT",
                    SourceTrial:  "DELIVER_SUBGROUP, EMPEROR-Preserved_SUBGROUP",
                })
            }


        case models.HFTypePreserved:
            if !activeSet["SGLT2i"] {
                gaps = append(gaps, MandatoryMedGap{
                    MissingClass: "SGLT2i",
                    Rationale:    "SGLT2i is the ONLY proven disease-modifying therapy for HFpEF",
                    Urgency:      "IMMEDIATE",
                    SourceTrial:  "EMPEROR-Preserved, DELIVER",
                })
            }
        }
    }


    return gaps
}

// kb-26-metabolic-digital-twin/internal/services/ckm_mhri_adjustment.go
package services


import "kb-metabolic-digital-twin/internal/models"


// MHRISubstageAdjustment modifies MHRI domain weights and score interpretation
// based on CKM substage.
type MHRISubstageAdjustment struct {
    GlucoseDomainWeight    float64 `json:"glucose_domain_weight"`
    CardioDomainWeight     float64 `json:"cardio_domain_weight"`
    BodyCompDomainWeight   float64 `json:"body_comp_domain_weight"`
    BehavioralDomainWeight float64 `json:"behavioral_domain_weight"`
    ScoreCeiling           float64 `json:"score_ceiling"`    // max possible MHRI score given severity
    InterpretationNote     string  `json:"interpretation_note"`
}


// Default MHRI weights: glucose 35%, cardio 25%, body_comp 25%, behavioral 15%


func ComputeCKMSubstageAdjustment(stage models.CKMStageValue, metadata *models.SubstageMetadata) MHRISubstageAdjustment {
    // Default weights — no adjustment for Stages 0-3
    adj := MHRISubstageAdjustment{
        GlucoseDomainWeight:    0.35,
        CardioDomainWeight:     0.25,
        BodyCompDomainWeight:   0.25,
        BehavioralDomainWeight: 0.15,
        ScoreCeiling:           100.0,
    }


    switch stage {
    case models.CKMStage4a:
        // Subclinical CVD: increase cardio weight — prevention is paramount
        adj.CardioDomainWeight = 0.30
        adj.GlucoseDomainWeight = 0.30
        adj.BodyCompDomainWeight = 0.25
        adj.BehavioralDomainWeight = 0.15
        adj.InterpretationNote = "Stage 4a: MHRI cardio domain weighted for subclinical CVD monitoring"


    case models.CKMStage4b:
        // Post-ASCVD: cardio domain dominates
        adj.CardioDomainWeight = 0.35
        adj.GlucoseDomainWeight = 0.30
        adj.BodyCompDomainWeight = 0.20
        adj.BehavioralDomainWeight = 0.15
        adj.ScoreCeiling = 85.0 // post-event patients can't reach "optimal" — inherent residual risk
        adj.InterpretationNote = "Stage 4b: MHRI reflects secondary prevention status; ceiling at 85 due to residual ASCVD risk"


    case models.CKMStage4c:
        if metadata == nil {
            adj.ScoreCeiling = 70.0
            adj.InterpretationNote = "Stage 4c: HF present but subtype unknown — conservative ceiling"
            return adj
        }


        switch metadata.HFClassification {
        case models.HFTypeReduced:
            adj.CardioDomainWeight = 0.40
            adj.GlucoseDomainWeight = 0.25
            adj.BodyCompDomainWeight = 0.20
            adj.BehavioralDomainWeight = 0.15
            adj.InterpretationNote = "Stage 4c HFrEF: cardio domain dominant — track EF, NT-proBNP, functional status"


        case models.HFTypeMildlyReduced:
            adj.CardioDomainWeight = 0.35
            adj.GlucoseDomainWeight = 0.25
            adj.BodyCompDomainWeight = 0.20
            adj.BehavioralDomainWeight = 0.20
            adj.InterpretationNote = "Stage 4c HFmrEF: balanced cardio + behavioral monitoring"


        case models.HFTypePreserved:
            adj.CardioDomainWeight = 0.25
            adj.GlucoseDomainWeight = 0.25
            adj.BodyCompDomainWeight = 0.25
            adj.BehavioralDomainWeight = 0.25 // equal weighting — HFpEF is a phenotypic syndrome
            adj.InterpretationNote = "Stage 4c HFpEF: equal domain weighting — obesity, exercise capacity, and comorbidity management drive outcomes"
        }


        // NYHA class ceiling
        if metadata.NYHAClass != "" {
            switch metadata.NYHAClass {
            case "IV":
                adj.ScoreCeiling = 30.0
            case "III":
                adj.ScoreCeiling = 50.0
            case "II":
                adj.ScoreCeiling = 70.0
            case "I":
                adj.ScoreCeiling = 85.0
            }
        }
    }


    return adj
}
