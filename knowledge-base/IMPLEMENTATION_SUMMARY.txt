Warfarin + Ciprofloxacin (INT-WARF-CIPRO-001):
  Severity: MAJOR
  Mechanism: CYP2C9 inhibition
  Effect: Increased INR (30-100%), bleeding risk
  Management: Reduce warfarin 30-50%, monitor INR q2-3 days
  Evidence: PMIDs 17011204, 15383697

Piperacillin-Tazobactam + Vancomycin (INT-PIPT-VANCO-001):
  Severity: MODERATE
  Mechanism: Additive nephrotoxicity
  Effect: Acute kidney injury
  Management: Monitor creatinine daily, adjust doses
  Evidence: PMIDs 27097733, 26786929

Opioids + Benzodiazepines (INT-OPIOID-BENZO-001):
  Severity: MAJOR
  Mechanism: Additive CNS depression
  Effect: Severe respiratory depression, death
  Management: Avoid if possible, continuous monitoring
  Evidence: PMID 29396945
